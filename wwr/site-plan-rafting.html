Overview

Purpose

The Adventure Rapids website aims to provide an informative and engaging platform for individuals interested in white water rafting. The site will offer details on available rafting trips, safety guidelines, FAQs, and booking options to help users plan their adventure with confidence.

Audience

Our target audience includes outdoor enthusiasts, families, and adventure seekers between the ages of 18-45 who are interested in white water rafting experiences. These individuals are looking for professional guidance, trip recommendations, and safety information before booking their rafting excursions. Most users will access the site via mobile devices or desktops while researching adventure trips.
